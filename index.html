Welcome to Tyre Guard

An intelligent real-time tyre monitoring and management system for dumpers.

Tyre Guard uses cutting-edge IIoT hardware and a mobile app to monitor tyre pressure, load, temperature, and location. This ensures optimal performance, safety, and cost-efficiency in mining operations by reducing the risk of tyre-related incidents and improving fuel efficiency.
